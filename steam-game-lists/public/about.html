About

Steam Lists allows you to look up your game library, see details on the games you own, and create lists. You can also see some insights on your favorite games and receive recommendations for new games.

User Guide

  • Input your Steam ID or vanity URL into the search bar, and press Enter or Search
  • If valid, your game library will show up. If not, make sure you are inputting either a 16-digit Steam ID or a valid vanity URL
  • To create a list, select games with the 'Select' button and provide a list title.
  • Your created lists can be seen at the bottom of the page, and you can remove games from your list or delete your list with the red trash can button.
  • You can view your favorite games, top genre, and get recommended a new game on the 'Recommendations' page.
