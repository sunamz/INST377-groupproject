About Steam Lists

Welcome to Steam Lists - your personal gaming companion! Our platform helps you organize, analyze, and discover games in your Steam library. Whether you're a casual gamer or a dedicated enthusiast, Steam Lists provides powerful tools to enhance your gaming experience.

Key Features

Library Management

Access and organize your entire Steam game collection in one place, with detailed information about each title.

Custom Lists

Create personalized game collections - perfect for organizing "Games to Play", "Favorites", or any category you imagine.

Gaming Insights

Discover patterns in your gaming habits, including your most-played genres and favorite developers.

Smart Recommendations

Get personalized game suggestions based on your gaming history and preferences.

Getting Started

Finding Your Steam ID

Enter either your 16-digit Steam ID or your vanity URL in the search bar. Your vanity URL can be found in your Steam profile URL after "steamcommunity.com/id/".

Creating and Managing Lists

  • Select games using the 'Select' button and choose a meaningful title for your list
  • View and manage your lists at the bottom of the page
  • Share your curated game lists with friends

Discovering New Games

  • Visit the 'Recommendations' page for personalized game suggestions
  • Explore your gaming statistics and patterns
